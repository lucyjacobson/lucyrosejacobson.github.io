It's a Secret
As Digital Designer for Baked by Melissa, I designed daily newsletter campaigns aligned with product launches and seasonal campaigns, while also creating cohesive visual campaigns across paid social advertisements, print materials, and landing pages. I led the redesign of the newsletter layout and visual style that increased engagement and revenue.

TIMELINE
January 2023 - June 2023

Brand Design

I focused on making the design for It’s a Secret instantly recognizable and impactful. The look uses a sharp monochromatic base of black and off-white and is contrasted with a single, highly saturated red. Paired with clean, utilitarian typography, the final identity is grunge, yet refined.

Flyer Design

It’s a Secret threw events every few weeks. I was tasked with designing social media flyers that were bold and scroll-stopping while clearly communicating important details like time, place, and the performers.

Merch Design

I designed a collection of merch, including a t-shirt, stickers, key chain, and notebook. The collection uses bold graphic placement and tactile quality to turn simple tokens into physical extensions of the brand's aesthetic.
