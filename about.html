About Me

Hi, I'm Lucy. I was born in Nashville, Tennessee, and have been on the move along the East Coast and abroad ever since. At age 7, I drove my pink Barbie Corvette off a ramp, and my love for risk-taking was born (though my Mom and Dad weren’t thrilled). I have a French Bulldog named Oliver, who I love more than anything. I am always happy to share photos of him.

I graduated from the Savannah College of Art and Design with a degree in advertising and branding, and recently got my master’s in interactive digital media from Trinity College Dublin. I’m most proud of designing and launching MUSA, a site that brings Irish Sign Language tours to museums and cultural institutions. Ask me more about it! Feel free to contact me with questions, opportunities, or if you just want to talk design.
