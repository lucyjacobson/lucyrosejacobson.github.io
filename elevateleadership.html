Elevate Leadership
Elevate Leadership Coaching, founded by Edna Gelsinger, empowers business leaders and organizations in the DC area to build resilient, high-performing teams. Their services include executive coaching, team development, and leadership and professional growth programs.

I was brought on to refresh their brand identity — redesigning the logo, color palette, and overall visual style — and to extend the new look across their website, social media, presentations, and promotional banners.

Brand Refresh

As Elevate Leadership matured, there was a recognized need to evolve its visual identity, specifically the logo and website, to better reflect the warmth and human-centric nature of its core ethos. The existing branding, while functional, lacked the personalized and inviting tone necessary to truly symbolize the company's mission: helping businesses and individuals work together to achieve their greatest potential in the workplace.

The challenge was to take this existing equity and elevate it to modern standards, creating a warmer, more symbolic style. This refresh successfully transformed the visual presence, making the brand feel more personalized and inviting and aligning its look with its deep commitment to collaborative growth.
